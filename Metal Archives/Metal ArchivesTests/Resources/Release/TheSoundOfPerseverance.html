Music by Chuck Schuldiner.

Logo redesigned by Strain.

Some of the songs on The Sound of Perseverance were originally going to appear on the first Control Denied album. When Death was signed to Nuclear Blast Chuck agreed to make one last Death album before moving forward with Control Denied.

Japanese version released by Victor.
