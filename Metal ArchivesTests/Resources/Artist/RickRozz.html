Rick Rozz

Real/full name:
Frederick DeLillo
Age:
54 (born Jan 9th, 1967)
Place of origin:
United States (New York City, New York)
Gender:
Male

Biography

One of the founding members of both Mantas/Death and Massacre.

Trivia

In a 1991 interview with Metal Forces magazine, Rick claims he was fired from Death by their manager, Eric Greif, over the phone, after he had left Florida for a few weeks to spend time with a girl in New Jersey, whom he had met while on tour with Death.
